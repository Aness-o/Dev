Sustainable cities and human settlements

Today, the world faces many environmental, economic, and social problems that affect people's lives and hinder sustainable development. Among these issues, cities play a major role in facing many challenges, such as traffic congestion, air and water pollution, waste accumulation, energy and natural resource shortages. Hence, the idea of sustainable cities came as a comprehensive solution to overcome these problems, aiming to create safe, healthy, and sustainable human communities environmentally, socially, and economically. This promotes human life in line with the sustainability of natural resources. Thus, sustainable cities are based on sustainability principles and adopt a comprehensive approach that includes economic, social, and environmental aspects. The three factors are considered in a single framework, and decisions are made according to this comprehensive concept.
